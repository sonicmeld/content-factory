# Content Factory

## Project Identity

Content Factory is a YouTube Content Operations Platform.

The system is designed to manage content assets, metadata, scheduling, and publishing across multiple YouTube channels.

---

## Content Factory IS

* Multi Channel YouTube Manager
* Asset Library Manager
* Prompt Library Manager
* Metadata Manager
* Upload Queue Manager
* YouTube Publisher

---

## Content Factory IS NOT

* AI Chat Platform
* AI Assistant
* Agent Framework
* Coding Assistant
* Workflow Builder
* Video Renderer
* AI Content Generator

---

## Content Creation Boundary

Content Factory does NOT generate:

* Videos
* Footage
* Images
* Audio
* Titles
* Descriptions

These assets are created outside the system.

Examples:

* 9Router AI Infrastructure
* External AI Services
* Manual Production Workflows

---

## Primary Business Entity

The primary business entity of Content Factory is a Content Package.

A Content Package represents one complete YouTube upload unit.

Example:

Sleep Sounds #001

* video.mp4
* thumbnail.webp
* description.txt
* timestamp.txt

Everything in the system exists to support Content Packages.

---

## Core Workflow

External Content Creation
↓

Content Package
↓

Upload Queue
↓

Channel Assignment
↓

Scheduling
↓

YouTube Publishing

---

## Asset Library Purpose

Asset Library is a supporting component.

It stores reusable:

* Footage
* Audio
* Prompts
* Thumbnail Assets

Asset Library is NOT the operational center of the platform.

---

## Operational Center

The operational center of Content Factory is the Upload Queue.

The Upload Queue connects:

* Content Packages
* Channels
* Schedules
* Publishing

---

## Mission

Manage content distribution efficiently across multiple YouTube channels from a single platform.

## Relationship with 9Router AI Infrastructure

Content Factory is designed to work alongside 9Router AI Infrastructure.

9Router provides access to multiple AI providers and content generation workflows.

Examples:

* Footage Generation
* Thumbnail Generation
* Metadata Generation
* Prompt Generation

The outputs generated through 9Router become reusable assets and content packages inside Content Factory.

Content Factory focuses on:

* Asset Organization
* Metadata Organization
* Content Packaging
* Upload Scheduling
* Multi Channel Distribution

Together:

9Router AI Infrastructure
↓
Content Creation
↓
Content Factory
↓
Content Operations & Distribution
